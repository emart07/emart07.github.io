Hello! Welcome to my art portfolio. I hope you will enjoy my art! Thank you for looking!

This series is about landscape drawings. The materials that I used for these images was a fine tip brush pen on sketching paper. The image on the left is inspired from a small park in east L.A. and the second drawing is based on some houses near the small park. Both images were done in the year 2020, at the beginning of COVID-19 happened. My inspiration for these drawings came from the situation that we had to stay indoors when covid 19 was hapening. The small colored boxes are a represenation of looking thru a window.

These are drawings that I was drawing while visiting these places. The left drawing is based on Little Tokyo in Down Town L.A. abd the next drawing is based on Long Beach. A beach near the Aquarium of the Pacific. The materials that I used was a black ink brush pen and a watercolor paper.

The materials that I used for the picture on the left was a black brush pen and watercolor paper. I drew this because I thought that the building and palm tree looked intreasting. The picture on the right was done with acrylics and bristol paper.
